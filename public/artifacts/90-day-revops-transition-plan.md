# 90-Day RevOps Transition Plan

## Days 1-30: Diagnose
- Map system topology and handoff ownership
- Audit critical data quality and SLA compliance
- Document top execution bottlenecks and business risk

## Days 31-60: Architect
- Prioritize high-leverage workflow redesigns
- Define governance model and decision rights
- Launch quick-win automation with guardrails

## Days 61-90: Operationalize
- Roll out manager and operator enablement
- Lock recurring review cadences and scorecards
- Confirm measurable outcomes and next-quarter roadmap
