Forecast quality is rarely a dashboard problem. It is usually an operating-system problem.

When leaders say forecasting feels "subjective," what they are really describing is inconsistency in stage criteria, ownership, and inspection behavior.

## Build the system, not just the report

A reliable forecast motion needs four components:

1. Explicit stage evidence requirements
2. Shared risk taxonomy
3. Manager action loops tied to exception handling
4. Weekly cadences that reinforce accountability

## What changes in practice

- Leaders review confidence with one language across teams
- Managers coach against concrete risk signals
- Reps get clear expectations instead of moving targets

## What to measure

- Stage aging by segment
- Close-date movement frequency
- Exception resolution rate
- Manager follow-through on risk-tagged deals

Forecast integrity improves when inspection criteria become behavioral norms, not one-time cleanup projects.
