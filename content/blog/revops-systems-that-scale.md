Scaling RevOps is less about adding tools and more about reducing ambiguity across teams.

## Key principles

- Shared stage definitions across funnel reporting.
- Tight ownership on lifecycle triggers.
- Operational metrics tied to business outcomes.

When teams share the same model, automation becomes reliable and reporting becomes actionable.
