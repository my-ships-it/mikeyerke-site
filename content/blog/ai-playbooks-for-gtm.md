Most GTM teams adopt AI in fragmented pockets first. The better approach is to define one operating model and then layer workflows into it.

## The approach

1. Define a single source of truth for customer data.
2. Automate repetitive prep work before automating decisions.
3. Start with one measurable workflow and prove it end-to-end.

## Where this works best

- Lead routing
- Meeting prep and account research
- Pipeline hygiene and forecasting support

AI compounds when it is connected to process and ownership, not just prompts.
