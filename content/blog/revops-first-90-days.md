The first 90 days in a RevOps leadership role should create operating confidence, not just discovery documentation.

## Days 1-30: Diagnose

- Map the current system topology and ownership boundaries
- Identify workflow breakpoints with the highest revenue risk
- Establish baseline metrics for signal quality and execution latency

## Days 31-60: Architect

- Prioritize and sequence improvements by risk and business impact
- Define governance model for process, tooling, and data changes
- Launch focused quick wins that prove operating momentum

## Days 61-90: Operationalize

- Lock recurring management cadences and scorecards
- Publish SOPs and handoff standards for team adoption
- Confirm measured impact and align next-quarter roadmap

The strongest 90-day plans balance short-term wins with architecture decisions that scale.
